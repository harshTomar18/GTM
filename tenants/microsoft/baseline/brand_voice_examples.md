# Brand Voice — Acme (example)

## What "good" sounds like

> Close the month in 3 days, not 12. Acme replaces fragmented spreadsheets with one
> workflow that auditors can follow without asking a single follow-up question.

## What "bad" sounds like (do not use)

- "Next-generation cloud-native finance platform" — banned phrases, vague claim.
- "Revolutionary AI-powered close" — overstatement, banned phrase.

## Sentence patterns to prefer

- Lead with the outcome ("Close in 3 days, not 12").
- Quantify when possible ("75% fewer reconciliation tickets").
- Name the team feeling it ("for finance ops managers who own month-end close").

## Sentence patterns to avoid

- Buzzword stacks.
- Vague comparatives ("better", "faster", "smarter") without a number.
- Apologetic hedges in CTAs ("If you have a moment, perhaps we could discuss...").
